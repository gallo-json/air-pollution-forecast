All times shown are in local standard time unless otherwise indicated.
7989         <p>
7990          Following EPA reporting guidelines, negative values may be displayed in our hourly criteria air quality data, down to the negative of the EPA listed Method Detection Limit (MDL) for the particular instrument that made the measurements. Prior to 1/1/2013, slightly negative values were automatically set to zero.
